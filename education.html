Education

Let's embark on an educational journey together.

Masters in Data Science - University of San Francisco

I opted for the one-year MS in Data Science program at the University of San Francisco for its unique practicum, offering hands-on industry experience. The Bay Area's tech-rich environment provides ample growth prospects. This program perfectly balances theory and practice, aligning with my career aspirations.

B.Tech(IT) - Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology, Delhi

I joined the graduate program at MAIT Delhi without a clear path, exploring various interests from web design to competitive coding and even acting. It was during an internship as a data analyst that I discovered my passion for data science. The guidance and mentorship I received there revealed my true potential, igniting a singular goal in my life.
Because my path was not clear in the beginning, I faced multiple setbacks and academic challenges. However, once I found my clear goal, everything changed. With newfound determination, I overcame those challenges and successfully progressed through my college journey scoring a decent CGPA in the end.

High School - VPS, Delhi

High school - most hated back then, but now, it's the time I miss the most. It was undoubtedly one of the best years of my life, incomparable to any other moment.

Those assembly lines, having lunch in the middle of class, receiving scolding and punishments from teachers, sneaking in firecrackers (feels like smuggling) during Diwali, the walk back home after school, using the excuse of working at the fest just to roam around, and even bunking mid-school classes after attendance just to catch a movie. And who could forget those parent-teacher meetings? Wish to feel that time once again.
